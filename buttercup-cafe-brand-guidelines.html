Clear Space Rule
Always maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the letter "B" in Buttercup around all sides of the logo.
Minimum Size
Primary logo: no smaller than 80px wide (digital) or 25mm wide (print). Submark: no smaller than 24px / 8mm diameter.

Backgrounds & Packaging Coastal Blue wraps the brand in a breezy, airy lightness — ideal for cups, bags, and digital surfaces.
Logo & Typography Deep Teal grounds the brand with depth and confidence. Use for all primary type and logo lockups.
Accents & Highlights Butter Yellow adds sunshine and energy — perfect for icons, stickers, and decorative details.
